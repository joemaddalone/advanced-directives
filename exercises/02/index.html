Exercise 02 /exercises/02/app/catalog/catalog-image-directive.js

Solution
  1. In the compile phase of our catalog-image-directive update the template by appending "<img ng-src={ { item.img } } />" to the templateElement.
  2. In the preLink stage of our catalog-image-directive use our controllers fallbackImage method to replace any missing images .
  3. In the postLink stage of our catalog-image-directive add a class of 'onSale' to the 'elm' of any items that are in fact on sale.
    Hint: scope.item will change by the time we can detect this
